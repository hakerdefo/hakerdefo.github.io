Take me to a place  
of mountain streams  
No sirens & screams  
and no pandemic  
Take me there my dream  
Take me there my dream  

Take me to a place  
with open views  
No more ICUs  
and no pandemic  
Take me there my dream  
Take me there my dream  

Take me to a place  
of rolling waves  
No more mass graves  
and no pandemic  
Take me there my dream  
Take me there my dream  

Take me to a place  
where I can be free  
No locks & keys  
and no pandemic  
Take me there my dream  
Take me there my dream
